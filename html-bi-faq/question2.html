If Blount Island has restricted access, how do I go about getting proper authorization to get on the island? When should I apply for my badges? Will I have to pay for it?

Access to Blount Island is restricted to only those who have a business purpose and can pass a background check. A TWIC badge includes a background check, and a JaxPort badge proves you have a business purpose. Information about the application process is under Site Requirements > Site Access. You must have supervisor authorization prior to obtaining these badges. Once approved, you will be reimbursed for the badge expense. Back
